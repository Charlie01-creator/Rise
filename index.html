About RISE AFRICA

At RISE AFRICA, we are committed to improving the lives of suffering children across Africa. Many children face extreme poverty, lack of access to education, and inadequate healthcare. With your support, we can provide them with the resources they need to thrive. Together, we can make a difference.

How Your Donations Will Be Used

Your donations will be used for:

  • 30%: Education and school supplies
  • 20%: Healthcare and medical supplies
  • 10%: Community development programs
